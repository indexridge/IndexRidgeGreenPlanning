# Legal and Copyright Risk Policy

This is an operational risk-control policy, not legal advice.

## Can this be zero-risk?

No. No business can be made genuinely free of liability or litigation risk if it publishes material, sells products, processes payments, sends marketing, or operates accounts in a person's name.

The goal is to keep risk low by using open public data, clear attribution, careful disclaimers, no copied creative material, no regulated advice, no personal-data enrichment, and no unsupported claims.

## Copyright / database-right rules

Allowed by default:

- Public facts from official/open datasets.
- Dataset fields obtained from sources with explicit open licences, such as Open Government Licence v3.0.
- Short factual summaries generated from records.
- Links back to official source records/pages.
- Licence and attribution text required by the source.

Not allowed without explicit review/permission:

- Copying news articles, blog posts, reports, images, screenshots, maps, PDFs, charts, paid-platform records, or proprietary databases.
- Reselling a third-party dataset if its licence does not allow reuse/commercial reuse.
- Scraping sites that prohibit scraping or republication in their terms.
- Using logos, brand assets, photos, or council portal screenshots in marketing.
- Building contact lists from personal data or sending unsolicited bulk marketing without compliance review.

## Public wording boundaries

Use:

- “Public-data monitoring”
- “Open-data digest”
- “Indicative planning-application records”
- “Links to official sources for verification”
- “AI-assisted filtering and summarisation”

Avoid:

- “Official”
- “Complete UK coverage”
- “Verified by humans”
- “Guaranteed leads”
- “Planning advice”
- “Investment advice”
- “Regulatory-ready”
- “Suitable for due diligence”
- “Guaranteed ROI”

## Mandatory disclaimer

> IndexRidge Green Planning Intelligence is an informational public-data monitoring product. It is not planning, legal, investment, engineering, environmental, or regulatory advice. Records may be incomplete, delayed, duplicated, incorrectly classified, or missing context. Users must verify all information against the relevant official source before acting.

## Launch gate

Do not publicly launch, sell, email prospects, buy ads, or upload to a marketplace until:

1. Source register is complete.
2. Licence/attribution is present for every source.
3. Sample output has been reviewed for copied text beyond factual source fields.
4. Public pages include the mandatory disclaimer.
5. The user approves the exact public copy and platform.
