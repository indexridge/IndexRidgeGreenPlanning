What it monitors

Planning records with renewable-energy keywords including solar, photovoltaic/PV, battery/BESS, EV charging, heat pumps, retrofit, substations, and grid connections.

Who it is for

Installers, consultants, analysts, sales teams, local suppliers, and researchers who want early public signals without manually searching every source.

Beta status

The current sample uses official open data and is intended to validate usefulness before any paid product, outreach, or broader source coverage is launched.

Low-risk data policy

  • Use open/public datasets with explicit licence and attribution.
  • Do not copy news articles, reports, images, maps, paid-platform data, or screenshots.
  • Do not claim official status, full UK coverage, guaranteed accuracy, guaranteed leads, or regulatory suitability.
  • Link users back to official sources for verification.
